Welcome to the Official Website for the Pharis Garden Centre

At the Pharis Garden Centre, we believe that a beautiful garden is the heart of a happy home.
Nestled in the heart of North Antrim, our garden centre offers a wide variety of plants, flowers, and gardening supplies to help you create your own green oasis.
Whether you're a seasoned gardener or just starting out, our friendly and knowledgeable staff are here to provide expert advice and personalized service.

Explore our extensive selection of seasonal plants, vibrant flowers, and unique garden accessories.
From organic fertilizers to stylish planters, we have everything you need to nurture your garden and bring your outdoor space to life.
Join us for workshops, events, and tips on sustainable gardening practices that will help you grow a thriving garden all year round.

Visit us today and discover the joy of gardening at the Pharis Garden Centre. Let's grow together!
