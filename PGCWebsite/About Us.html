About Us

Welcome to Pharis Garden Centre! We are passionate about plants and dedicated to helping you create the garden of your dreams.
Our team of experts is here to provide you with the best advice, products, and services to make your gardening experience enjoyable and successful.
Founded in 2016, Pharis Garden Centre has grown from a small family business to a beloved community hub. We pride ourselves on our wide selection of plants,
gardening tools, and outdoor decor. Whether you're a seasoned gardener or just starting out, we have something for everyone.
Visit us today and discover why Pharis Garden Centre is the go-to destination for all your gardening needs!
